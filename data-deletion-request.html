Account and Data Deletion Policy

At overloadChamp, we respect your privacy and data rights. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data from our systems.

Data We Collect

overloadChamp collects and stores the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication details
  • User Preferences: Weight unit preference (kg/lbs) and app settings
  • Workout Data: Your custom workouts, exercises, weights, and progress

Deletion Process

To request deletion of your account and data, please complete and submit the form below. We will process your request within 14 days and send a confirmation email when completed.

Data Retention

Upon deletion request, we will:

  • Immediately delete: All workout data, progress history, and personal settings
  • Retain temporarily: Basic account information and email address for 30 days to prevent accidental deletion and comply with legal requirements
  • Anonymize: Any usage analytics data by removing all personal identifiers
