Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FeedChamp ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Engineer Champloo LLC (doing business as webChamploo), collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our Discord bot service, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Register for and configure the FeedChamp Discord bot
  • Submit social media account usernames to monitor (Twitter/X and TikTok)
  • Configure Discord channels for content delivery
  • Purchase a subscription
  • Contact us for support

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Discord server IDs where the bot is installed
  • Discord channel IDs where content is delivered
  • Discord server owner IDs
  • Social media account names that you configure for monitoring
  • Timestamps of social media content checks
  • IDs of content that has been posted to your Discord server

1.3 Payment Information

When you purchase a subscription, your payment information is collected and processed by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. We do not store complete credit card information on our servers. Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Monitor and deliver social media content to your Discord server
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Develop new features and services
  • Protect against misuse of our Services

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share information about you as follows:

  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf. This includes our payment processor (Stripe) and cloud infrastructure providers.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to (a) comply with laws, regulations, legal process, or government requests; (b) protect the safety, rights, or property of FeedChamp, our users, or the public; and (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your consent or at your direction.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain the information we collect about you for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including maintaining your active account and subscription. When you delete your account or remove our bot from your Discord server, we will delete or anonymize your information within a reasonable period, unless we need to retain certain information for legitimate business or legal purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Account Information: You can update or delete the social media accounts you monitor through our Discord bot commands.
  • Bot Removal: You can remove the FeedChamp bot from your Discord server at any time, which will stop all data collection for that server.
  • Access and Correction: You can request access to your personal information or request corrections by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required for social media usage in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us so we can remove the information.

7. International Data Transfers

We are based in the United States and the information we collect is governed by U.S. law. If you are accessing our Services from outside the United States, please be aware that information collected through the Services may be transferred to, processed, stored, and used in the United States and other jurisdictions. By using the Services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States and other countries where data protection laws may be different from or less comprehensive than those in your country.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and notify users through the Discord bot or our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our information practices.
